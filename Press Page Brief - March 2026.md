## **3\. Messaging Alignment**

All copy on the page should reflect Skillvue's updated English positioning (not a literal translation of the Italian site). Key principles from the product marketing doc:

* **What Skillvue is:** An AI-powered talent intelligence platform that adds a dynamic, objective dimension to HR data — transforming static information into predictive insights across hiring, performance management, internal mobility, and L\&D.  
* **Hero statement:** "Skillvue brings people processes to life — turning static HR processes into predictive intelligence that makes every talent decision objective and defensible."  
* **Category:** AI-Powered Talent Decision Platform (not "HR tech startup" or "assessment tool").  
* **Key differentiators to reinforce if copy allows:**  
  * Science \+ AI combined (psychometric rigour \+ LLM-powered generation)  
  * Customised to each company's own competency model  
  * Full talent lifecycle coherence (one framework, four use cases)  
  * Transformation Readiness Assessment (unique capability)  
  * European enterprise-native (GDPR \+ EU AI Act compliant by design)  
* **Tone:** Confident, enterprise-grade, evidence-driven. Not startup-scrappy. Think "trusted intelligence partner" not "cool new tool."  
* **Avoid:** Generic HR-tech language like "streamline your hiring process" or "skills partner for soft and technical skills assessment" (this is the older en.skillvue.ai messaging — the new positioning is sharper and more C-suite oriented).
